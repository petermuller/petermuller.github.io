About

My name is Peter Muller, and welcome to my site! Here you can find some of the projects that I've worked on and want to show off to you. I enjoy taking on challenging projects in order to learn new skills and technologies. Feel free to explore and make yourself at home. For my professional resume, please visit my LinkedIn page.
